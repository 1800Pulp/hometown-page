Austin,Tx

Enjoy the vibrant and weird culture Austin has to offer. Between festivals, live music daily, to the amazing river and trails.

My top three favorite free activities

Walk a trail and get a superb view

Mount bonnell is a short trail with an absolutely beautiful view of downtown on one side, and a view of the wonderful lady bird lake on another.

Take a day off on the lake

The lake is a great place to relax and enjoy the beautiful scenery. There are many places to paddle around to. You can be surrounded by people in one spot, or completely isolated in another

Enjoy a festival

Festivals are a great way to get to the city and enjoy what it has to offer. There are many festivals in Austin, and you can find one that suits your schedule or interests.
